# MUI.COM.UA

Digital platform showcasing manufacturers and craftsmen to buyers and partners.

## Features

- **Registration & profiles** — onboarding for individuals and companies, with profile and credential management.
- **Product catalogs** — structured catalog publishing.
- **Free search** — discovery of products and manufacturers.
- **Blog & newsfeed** — built-in publishing for updates and industry content.

## Value

Gives manufacturers and artisans the visibility and reach usually reserved for large enterprises.
